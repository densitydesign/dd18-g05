THE PROJECT

In 2022 private jets have become a relevant topic in the fight against climate change. Using #banprivatejets, groups of activists, politicians and scientists have begun to highlight the need to tax emissions of private jets more decisively or, more extremely, to ban them. The project has been carried out during the Final Synthesis Design Studio held by DensityDesign of the MSc in Communication Design, Politecnico di Milano. This web project is part of previous research published in a report.
